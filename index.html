HOW IT ALL STARTED

My Robotics Journey

In Academic Year 2019-2020, I took the opportunity to be exposed to Robotics. It was a memorable learning experience because I was one of the few students who qualified to participate in the program after a thorough assessment.

We were able to build a robot (EV3 Lego Mindstorm) and as a team, we were able to program it to do certain tasks.

We were also able to build a path in which the robot should follow. Creativity is indeed crucial in this part.

Robotics has taught me to think outside the box as programming could be quite tricky. It developed my problem solving and critical thinking skills.

During the Robotics Technolympics, I had the chance to lead my team. The task was to command and program the robot to follow the line as shown in the picture above. With full confidence and trust in my team, we were recognized as being the top 2.

It was indeed a roller coaster ride, the experience has ignited my passion for programming that brought me to where I am today.
